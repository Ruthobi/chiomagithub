An image compressor can reduce the file size of an image in different ways. Depending on the format you are choosing (JPG, PNG, or other), you can choose a compression level. This will affect the image quality as minimally as possible.

If your image is still too big, you can lower the file size by also lowering the quality of the image.

How to compress an image?

  1. Choose a file format from the dropdown menu.
  2. Choose a compression preset from the dropdown menu. It will determine the quality of your image.
  3. For further compression, you can also lower the quality of the image. (optional)
